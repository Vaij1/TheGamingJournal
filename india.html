Top 5 Video Games Developed In India

1.) Raaji:An Ancient Epic


On 16 April 2024 the game was released on the PlayStation Plus catalog in a new version for the PlayStation 5 which features graphic enhancements, raytracing, a Platinum Trophy and haptic feedback, lightbar and adaptive triggers for the DualSense Controller.[5]The game incorporates Hindu mythology and takes inspiration from epics such as the Mahabharata and Ramayana. Graphically, the game is designed in the style of Pahari paintings, and the in-game architecture is modeled after that of medieval Rajasthan.

2.) Mumbai Gullies


Explore a compelling story along with a strong character who has sacrificed a lot in life to reach the top. Enter Mumbai Gullies, a game where you would play as an Indian protagonist walking through the unique landscape of Mumbai. An open world action adventure game, where you will be carrying out missions to reach the top, from ruling local gullies of Mumbai to ruling the city itself. Experience the adrenaline rush as you drive fast through the action packed streets of Mumbai while evading cops.

3.) Kamla: The Horor Game



Kamla is a survival horror game that involves evading a dangerous evil entity while trying to perform an Indian ritual to exorcise it. You got trapped inside the mansion unexpectedly without any of the ceremonial items you had prepared. Now you must look for items in the house and improvise new plans to banish the spirit and escape. Find necessary recipe items for the ritual around the map while trying to evade Kamla and prevent being seen, Kamla chases you if she senses your presence close by. Use hiding spots to run and hide, Place the items in the courtyard as you find them one by one. Explore an authentic Indian Mansion (Haveli) set in the 70’s time period with authentic Indian environment. Kamla is inspired by various Indian folklore and local horror stories
